Capsule Bio

I am a Lecturer (equivalent to Assistant Professor in the US system) in the School of Engineering at the University of Glasgow, Glasgow, United Kingdom. Prior to joining Glasgow I was research fellow at École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne (EPFL), visiting research scholar at the University of California, Berkeley, and post-doctoral researcher at the Centre for Research and Technology Hellas. I received my Dipl.-Eng. degree in Industrial Engineering, and MSc, PhD degrees in Operations Research (specialising in the modelling, control and optimisation of large-scale transportation networks) from the Technical University of Crete, Greece.

Academic Service

  • Editorial Board, Transportation Research Procedia (since 2014)

  • Editorial Board, Transportation Research Part C: Emerging Technologies (since 2015)

Research Interests

  • Traffic flow modelling and simulation

  • Network management and control

  • Traffic signal control in large-scale urban road networks

  • Pervasive sensing and monitoring of traffic congestion and gridlock

  • Intelligent transportation systems

  • Operations research and mathematical programming

  • Optimal control systems
